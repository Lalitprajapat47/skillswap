How SkillSwap Works

Peer-to-peer learning in a few simple steps.

1. Sign Up

Create an account using your email or college ID.

2. Build Profile

Fill Skills Offered, Skills Wanted, level, and available time slots.

3. Get Matches

Matching engine finds students whose interests perfectly align with yours.

4. Swap Skills

Send or accept swap requests to start the learning partnership.

5. Learn & Teach

Conduct sessions through chat or external tools and share resources.

6. Track Progress

Mark sessions as completed and build your teaching/learning record.
